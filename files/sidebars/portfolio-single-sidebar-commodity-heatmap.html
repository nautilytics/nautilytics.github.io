Map of Commodity Futures Market

Context
Inspired by the Map of the Market at SmartMoney.com and my background in agricultural economics, I used the flexible treemap layout in d3.js to build a zoomable treemap/heatmap giving visitors to the site a quick overview of the metals, energies, and livestock commodity futures markets.
Data
Futures market prices are updated on load of application from: AgriCharts API.
Tech Notes
  • This visualization gives users the ability to view only those contracts nearest to expiration as well as sizing the nodes by either count (equal area), open interest, or volume.
  • This project gave me the opportunity to explore the ease of getting a project up and running on the Heroku infrastructure.
  • The front-end is built using jQuery and d3.js.
  • The back-end technologies are Node.js and Express.
